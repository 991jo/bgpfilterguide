BGP Large Communities are a novel way to signal information between networks. Large BGP Communities are easy to use, implement and deploy.

An example of Large Communities is: 2914:65400:38016. Large BGP Communities are composed of three 4-byte integers, separated by a colon. This is easy to remember and accommodates advanced routing policies in relation to 4-Byte ASNs.

This website brings together implementers and end users of the Large BGP Communities.
